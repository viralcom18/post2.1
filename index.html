What is Adsterra?

Adsterra is a global online advertising network that helps website owners and traffic publishers earn money by displaying high‑quality ads. Advertisers use the platform to promote apps, websites, and CPA offers worldwide.

Who Can Use Adsterra?

  • Website owners and bloggers
  • Landing page creators
  • Telegram and social media traffic owners
  • App developers
  • Affiliate marketers

How Adsterra Works

  1. Sign up as a publisher
  2. Add your website or generate a SmartLink
  3. Place ad code or share the link
  4. Visitors view ads or complete offers
  5. You earn money for valid traffic

Ad Formats Available

  • Popunder ads
  • Native banner ads
  • Display banner ads
  • Social bar ads
  • SmartLink (direct monetization link)

What is SmartLink?

  • Automatically shows the best‑paying offers
  • No website required
  • Perfect for Telegram, WhatsApp, and social traffic
  • Optimized for maximum revenue

Approval Process

  • Fast and simple account approval
  • No strict rules like Google AdSense
  • Works well for new websites and beginners

Advantages of Using Adsterra

  • Easy to get started
  • Works even with low traffic
  • Telegram‑friendly monetization
  • Reliable and fast payments
  • Multiple ad formats

Payment Methods

  • USDT (Cryptocurrency)
  • PayPal
  • Payoneer
  • Paxum
